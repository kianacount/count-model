What is CountGD?

CountGD is a multi-modal object counting model that utilizes prompts and visual examples to count objects with exceptional accuracy. It consistently delivers results within a 5% margin of error across complex scenarios with over 100 objects.

Important Clarification

CountGD is not a segmentation model. It focuses exclusively on accurate counting without needing to segment each individual object.

Potential Applications

  • Wildlife monitoring and conservation
  • Manufacturing quality control
  • Retail inventory analysis
  • Medical imaging and diagnostics
  • Material science and research
