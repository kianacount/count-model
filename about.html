What is CountGD?

CountGD is a multi-modal object counting model developed to deliver highly accurate object counts across a wide variety of domains. It uses prompts and visual examples, and maintains a margin of error under 5%, even in complex, real-world scenes.

How CountGD Works

Unlike segmentation models, CountGD focuses specifically on counting without requiring per-object segmentation. Users provide textual descriptions, visual examples, or a combination of both to guide the model toward accurate counting results.
