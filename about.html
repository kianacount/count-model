What is CountGD?

CountGD is a multi-modal object counting model developed by the Visual Geometry Group at the University of Oxford. It leverages both visual exemplars and text prompts to accurately count objects in diverse scenarios.

Learn more from the CountGD Paper and explore the Open-Source Code.
